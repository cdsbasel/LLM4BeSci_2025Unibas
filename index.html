Welcome

This is the home of the workshop Open-Source Large Language Models for Behavioral Science at the Faculty of Psychology, University of Basel, on February 10-12.

The three of the workshop will be dedicated a training (day 1) introducing the use of open LLMs, a symposium showcasing research project leveraging LLMs, and a project ideation session focused on developing new projects using LLMs.

Before attending the workshop, please visit the Installation page and follow the instructions.

Looking forward to a productive workshop on large language models in the behavioral sciences!

Zak Hussain, Rui Mata, Dirk Wulff
